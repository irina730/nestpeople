Employer of Record (EOR)

We provide U.S. Employer of Record (EOR) services that let overseas companies legally employ U.S. workers without setting up a U.S. entity. As the legal employer of record, we handle employment contracts, payroll, tax filing, benefits, Workers’ Compensation, HR compliance, and day-to-day employment administration — while you direct the work and manage performance.

We take on:

  • Employment Liability
  • Payroll & tax liability
  • Workers’ Comp
  • Employment Practices Liability (EPLI)
  • HR Compliance
  • Multi-state Compliance
  • Benefits Administration
